The Light of Christ

“I am the light of the world: he that followeth me shall not walk in darkness.” — John 8:12

Christ Teaches About Light

Jesus Christ taught that He is the light of the world. His light guides us, comforts us, and shows us the path back to Heavenly Father. When we follow Him, we are not lost in darkness but filled with hope.

“I am the light which ye shall hold up…” — 3 Nephi 18:24

Let Your Light Shine

Christ taught His disciples to let their light shine before the world.

When we serve others and keep the commandments, our light grows brighter.

“Ye are the light of the world… let your light so shine.” — Matthew 5:14–16

Change of Heart

Through the Light of Christ, we can change. The scriptures teach that when we come unto Him, our hearts are filled with light and we become new people.

“Have ye experienced this mighty change in your hearts?” — Alma 5:14

Walk in the Light

The Light of Christ is given to every person. As we follow Him, our lives become brighter, our burdens become lighter, and our path becomes clear.

“That which is of God is light.” — D&C 50:24
