Banana Oat Pancakes

  • (2–3 servings)
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 large egg (or flax egg)
  • ¼–⅓ cup applesauce
  • ½ cup dairy-free milk
  • ¼ tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp cinnamon (optional)
  • 1 tsp vanilla (optional)
  • Pinch of salt
Instructions
  1. Blend oats into flour
  2. Add banana, egg, applesauce, milk, baking soda, salt, extras
  3. Rest batter 2–3 min (let soda activate)
  4. Cook small pancakes in a lightly oiled pan over medium-low heat

Soy Garlic Chicken

  • 1½ lb chicken
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce (gluten-free if desired)
  • ¼ teaspoon dark soy sauce (optional)
  • ¼ teaspoon allspice
  • 2 cloves minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
Instructions
  1. Cook rice or noodles.
  2. Cut chicken into thin, bite-sized pieces.
  3. Whisk together soy sauce, dark soy sauce, allspice, garlic, and brown sugar.
  4. Heat olive oil and sesame oil in a skillet.
  5. Add chicken and brush with about half of the sauce mixture.
  6. Cook about 5 minutes, flip, and brush with remaining sauce.
  7. Add frozen vegetables and cook 7–9 minutes until chicken reaches 165°F.

Chocolate Chip Cookies

  • 2 ¼ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ¾ cup granulated sugar
  • ¾ cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 cups chocolate chips
Instructions
  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, beat butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until creamy.
  4. Add eggs and vanilla and mix well.
  5. Gradually mix in the dry ingredients.
  6. Stir in chocolate chips.
  7. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto a baking sheet.
  8. Bake 9–11 minutes until golden brown.
